# MA 43X - Rev. 10.5 (Public Beta)
(A) printable G l o c k 43X frame(s).

* DD43.1 Rail spec by nguyenkvvn,
* DD43.1 Negative/Jig by Phil Phisher, modified/refined by マクネー 武器.
* P80 Rail Spec by Polymer80 
* P80 Negative/Jig by Neutron Nick, modified/refined by マクネー 武器.

## Description
マクネー 武器's 43X is an all-original modeled, revisitation of the Glock 43X. The first release uses DD43.1, or PF9SS rail specifications.

## Materials Required
See included PDF for tools and parts checklist [BOM is for DD43, some of the guide still applies to the PF9SS version].

## Printing Instructions:
* Print the frame standing up, at an angle (probably the strongest orientation), or rails down. 
* Preferred filament should be PLA+, or BETTER (Nylon CF or Nylon CF blend is probably the strongest, at the moment). 
* Print at 50 mm/s @ 230C (eSun PLA+, for example). 
* Use tree supports for easy clean up.* (suggested, adjust to machine/filament).
* Print with at least six walls.
* See included PDF for assembly instructions.
